What are the main types of natural fibers?

Natural fibers are derived from plants and animals. Common examples include: Plant-based fibers: Cotton, jute, flax (linen) Animal-based fibers: Wool, silk

How are fibers transformed into fabric?

Spinning: Fibers are twisted together to form yarns. Weaving or Knitting: Yarns are interlaced (weaving) or looped (knitting) to create fabric.

What is the difference between woven and knitted fabrics?

Woven fabrics: Created by interlacing two sets of yarns at right angles. They are generally firm and have less stretch.

How often should bedding fabrics like duvet covers be washed?

t's recommended to wash duvet covers every one to two weeks. Regular washing removes sweat, oils, and dust mites, promoting better hygiene and skin health. Households with pets or children might consider washing them weekly
